Multitouch Bug Test

Many older Android devices, generally those shipped with Froyo (2.2) or earlier, have a significant bug in how multiple touch points (e.g. "multitouch") are handled.

This program is designed to demonstrate.

Performing the Test

Touch the screen several times at once. You should see a circle appear at each touch point, along with the ID number of the touch point, as reported by Android. As you hold a touch point, that point's ID number should not change.

On buggy devices, if you touch #1, touch #2, then while holding #2, lift and tap #1 repeatedly, you will notice the numbers on the two touch points swapping back and forth. This confirms the bug.
